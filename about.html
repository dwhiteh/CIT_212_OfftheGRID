1. You need land. Preferably not within sight of a Walmart.
2. Protection from the elements. Consider if you'll be building a house, sleeping in a tent, or possibly a camper.
3. You'll need to hydrate. We'd recommend a LifeStraw or some other way to collect water. Be sure to take appropriate precautions to ensure water is potable.
4. Food. You'll need guns. Or grow a garden. Or gather wild produce. Or go to Costco every other week.
5. Will you require power or will you be legitimately off the grid?
6. If you do need power, maybe consider packing batteries to store power you have generated.
7. How will you deal with your waste? Hopefully, responsibly.
8. And finally....do you really want to do this? It is difficult and you must be committed. Consider it carefully.
.....don't forget about the bears.
